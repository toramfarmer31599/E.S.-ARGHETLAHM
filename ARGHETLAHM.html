GUILD HISTORY

A neophyte player was randomly invited to a dead guild at the end of February 2017. The guild was a mere level 5 that had 20 inactive players which included it's own guild leaders. A week has passed without anybody logging in at all…

One evening, The golden crown was passed on to her… along with the responsibilities that the crown embodies…

The guild was finally abandoned… by its former masters…

Having no experience in handling a guild and being a new player as she was, she had all the right to escape such a burden. But unlike the guild's former leaders, She persevered and decided not to give up.

The very first person that she invited was the one whom she trusted the most and together they slowly built the guild's level till the guild bar became accessible. She then started inviting all of those in her very small friends' list as new members.

The new Guild Master was hands on and did everything to please new members. It was a heavy responsibility… yet it was something that she faced bravely because she wasn't alone anymore. There were those who stood by her side despite difficulties.

The guild's members grew in numbers… yet it also faced difficulties and suffered nasty rumors due to it's former members before the neophyte Guild Master handled its affairs.

It was then christened as "Arghetlahm" to embody the values and visions of all it's members that hailed from nations all throughout the world.

GUILD RULES




• Respect eachother, treat equally, racism is forbidden.

• Avoid begging for spinas/equipments/items... Although giving/lending stuffs isn't pohibited, please be reminded that the guild will not be held liable for any losses.

• Whenever you are in a party with guildmates or random players, always ask for permission or remind your party if you are going to change characters.

• Leeching is not prohibited but always ask if your party will allow you to leech.

• If you need help from guildmates, don't let them wait for you but rather you will the one who will wait for them in a certain map.

• Scamming is not allowed. If someone is caught with a solid proof, then they will automatically be kicked from the guild and labeled as a scammer.

• 7 Days inactivity without valid reasons is subject for removal from the guild either temporary or permanently depending on the circumstances.

• Breaking the rules will be given a warning. If caught caught again he/she will be given certain punishments.
